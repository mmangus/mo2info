Bow Damage Data

  1. Make sure your Archery skill is 100.
  2. Input your bow's type, range, current durability, and max durability below.
  3. Find a target dummy in town and stand about 1 horse length away (exact distance does not seem to matter).
  4. Shoot 10 fully-charged arrows into the head of the target dummy.
    • Use broadhead arrows for short and asymmetric bows. Use longbow arrows for longbows.
  5. Check your combat log, add up the total amount of damage you did, then divide by 10 to find your mean damage per shot.
