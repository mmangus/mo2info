Bow Damage Model Training

The goal of this tool is to build a model that can tell you how much damage a bow will do (on average to a target dummy) based on its attributes. But first, gotta collect the raw data. You can help by following this procedure:
  1. Make sure your Archery skill is trained to 100.
  2. Input your bow's type, range, current durability, and maximum durability below.
  3. Find a target dummy in town and stand about 1 horse length away.
    • In initial testing, distance from target had minimal (no?) effect on damage, at least at moderate distances, so it's probably not important that you do this precisely as long as you are close enough to reliably hit the dummy in the head.
  4. Shoot 10 fully-charged arrows into the head of the target dummy.
    • Use broadhead arrows for short or asymmetric bows and longbow arrows with longbows.
  5. Check your combat log, add up the total amount of damage you did, then divide by 10 to find your mean damage per shot.
